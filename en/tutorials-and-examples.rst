# Advanced Blog tutorial
########
    In this tutorial you will take your Blog to another adavnced aspect, in which, you will be able to do the following:
     * Creating your own Components for the blog controllers
     * Creating your own Behaviors for blog Models
     * Creating your own Helpers for the blog Views
     * Using comments plugin in your blog
     * Finally packging your blog as a plugin to use it in any free open source CakePHP based application.
